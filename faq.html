Answers

When was Stephen Colbert born?
Stephen Colbert was born on May 13, 1964, in Washington, D.C.
Where did Stephen Colbert go to school?
Stephen Colbert graduated from Porter-Gaud High School. He originally attended Hampden-Sydney College in Virginia as a philosophy major. After 2 years, he transferred to Northwestern University, where he graduated in 1986 with a Bachelor's of Arts in Communication.
What was Stephen Colbert's first job?
Stephen Colbert's first job was in the ticket office at The Second City in Chicago, Illinois.
What was the first TV show Stephen Colbert worked on?
The first TV show Stephen Colbert worked on was The Dana Carvey Show. He served with fellow Second City alum Steve Carell.
When did Stephen Colbert get his "big break"?
Stephen Colbert's first "big break" was in 1997, when he was hired as a writer and presenter on The Daily Show. His success there would launch his own TV show later.
When did Stephen Colbert get his own show?
Stephen Colbert became the host of his own show, The Colbert Report, in 2005 after his success on The Daily Show.
What does Stephen Colbert do now?
Stephen Colbert is the host, writer, and executive producer of The Late Show on CBS. He took over hosting duties from the legendary David Letterman in 2015. In addition, he has recently worked on a number of animated series in various roles.
How many siblings does Stephen Colbert have?
Stephen Colbert has 10 siblings. He is the youngest of 11 children.
Has Stephen Colbert won any awards?
Stephen Colbert has won several awards, including 10 Emmy Awards. He also won the 2010 Grammy Award for Best Comedy Album and a 2014 Grammy Award for Best Spoken Word Album.
What will Stephen Colbert do next?
Only he knows that!
